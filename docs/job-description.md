## Role Summary

The Senior Digital Full Stack Engineer is responsible for undertaking development activities required to deliver IT solutions that meet business needs. The role estimates and delivers systems development work to agreed time, budget and quality targets, in line with organisational standards. The role also resolves technical development issues throughout the full development lifecycle.

---

## Accountabilities

* Complete development deliverables in a timely manner and to the appropriate quality so that solutions are fit for purpose, supportable and adaptable to future change.
* Work with DD&T, the business and external partners to ensure solutions are delivered on time, on schedule and on budget.
* Collaborate with team members to provide delivery estimations.
* Work across DD&T to assess system requirements and specifications and identify potential issues prior to implementation.
* Design technical solutions for assigned initiatives.
* Assist and support testing activities (system, integration and user acceptance testing).
* Create and maintain application documentation to support operations and future change.
* Follow change control procedures and maintain version control and configuration management to protect production services.
* Report on development progress and take action on risks, issues and exceptions, escalating where required.
* Maintain up-to-date awareness of system security processes and procedures.
* Contribute to post-implementation reviews and incorporate learnings into future work.
* Maintain awareness of industry trends and technologies used within the business.
* Ensure all activities comply with information security standards.
* Consider customer needs and experience targets in all solutions.
* Recommend improvements to operational efficiency and standards.
* Act as a business change driver to improve value, productivity and efficiency.

## Key Selection Criteria

* 8+ years of industry experience or demonstrated capability and growth as a full stack engineer
* Strong expertise in:

  * TypeScript / Node.js backend development
  * AWS serverless architecture (Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB)
  * Infrastructure as Code using Terraform or CloudFormation
  * API design and implementation
  * Testing frameworks and methodologies
  * Security best practices and implementation
  * Git version control and CI/CD pipelines
* Proficiency in HTML5 and CSS3 to create responsive web interfaces
* Experience with at least one JavaScript framework such as Angular, Vue.js or React
* Strong awareness of OWASP Top 10 security risks and secure coding practices
* Hands-on experience using AWS or Azure SDKs and core services for cloud-native or hybrid applications
* Hands-on experience writing unit and integration tests, with a clear understanding of testing boundaries and code coverage
* Exposure to container technologies such as Docker and Kubernetes
* Familiarity with logging best practices for monitoring and troubleshooting large-scale systems
* Experience working in agile teams using Scrum or Kanban
* Experience with observability tools such as Splunk and AWS CloudWatch
